INTRODUCTION

Socotra is a botanical treasure chest unlike anywhere else on Earth.
Its long isolation has led to the evolution of a stunning array of
endemic plants—species found nowhere else.

here is a group of the most common endemic plants in Socotra :

  1. Dragon's Blood Tree:

    1 2 3
    html5 slideshow by WOWSlider.com v9.0

    OVERVIEW

    Appearance:
    Umbrella-shaped crown with dense foliage; looks like a giant mushroom from afar.
    Unique Trait:
    Produces a crimson resin known as “dragon's blood”,
    used historically as medicine, dye, and incense.
    Ecological Role:
    Its shape helps shade the roots and reduce evaporation
    an adaptation to Socotra's arid climate.

  2. Bottle Tree Adenium obesum socotranum :

    3. OVERVIEW

    Appearance:
    Swollen, bottle-like trunk with vibrant red-and-white flowers;
    grows up to 3 meters tall.
    Adaptation:
    Stores water in its thick trunk to survive long dry seasons.

  3. Frankincense Tree Boswellia sacra

    :

    4. OVERVIEW

    Appearance:
    Gnarled trunk with papery bark; grows 2-8 meters tall.
    Product:
    Yields aromatic resin (frankincense)
    used in perfumes, incense, and traditional medicine.
    Harvesting:
    Resin is collected by making small cuts in the bark;
    it hardens into fragrant granules
